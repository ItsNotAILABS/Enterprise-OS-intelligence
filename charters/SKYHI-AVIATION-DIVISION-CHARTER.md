## Mission & Mandate

### Mission Statement

Skyhi's mission is to be the **sovereign intelligence layer for the global aviation ecosystem** — the single platform that airports, airlines, booking platforms, and aviation workforce depend on for real-time operational intelligence, regulatory compliance, revenue optimization, and passenger care.

### Mandate

The Skyhi division is chartered to:

1. **Own the aviation intelligence market** — deploy RSHIP AGI technology into every major US airport and airline
2. **Enable multi-company connection** — any airline, airport, OTA, or TMC can connect via the VISITEX API
3. **Serve the full workforce** — from C-suite operations to pilots, flight attendants, and ground crew
4. **Maintain zero-violation safety standards** — FAA Part 117 compliance is non-negotiable
5. **Continuously upgrade** — add AGI organs as the aviation ecosystem demands new intelligence layers

### Why a Dedicated Division

Aviation is a domain with its own:
- **Regulatory framework** (FAA, ICAO, TSA, CBP) requiring specialized compliance knowledge
- **Technical standards** (IATA, ATA, OAG formats, GDS protocols)
- **Market dynamics** (hub-and-spoke economics, yield management, slot constraints)
- **Workforce structure** (union agreements, seniority systems, type ratings, duty rules)
- **Safety culture** (zero-tolerance for compliance failures)

A dedicated Skyhi division ensures this domain expertise compounds within a focused team rather than being diluted across general enterprise intelligence products.

## Tenant Ecosystem

### Design Principle

The Skyhi division is built around a **platform model, not a point solution model**. The VISITEX gateway transforms Skyhi into a hub that the entire aviation ecosystem connects to.

```
SKYHI PLATFORM HUB
        │
        ├── Airlines (API tenants)
        │     American Airlines, Delta, United, Southwest, JetBlue, Alaska, ...
        │
        ├── Airports (API tenants)
        │     DFW, ORD, ATL, LAX, JFK, MIA, SEA, SFO, ...
        │
        ├── Online Travel Agencies (API tenants)
        │     Expedia, Booking.com, Google Flights, Kayak, Priceline, ...
        │
        ├── Global Distribution Systems (API tenants)
        │     Travelport, Sabre, Amadeus, Galileo
        │
        └── Corporate TMCs (API tenants)
              Amex GBT, BCD Travel, CWT, FCM, TravelEdge, ...
```

Each category of tenant has a specialized value proposition:

**Airlines:** Replace legacy CMS, FRMS, and revenue management. Pay per seat/per month.  
**Airports:** Replace gate management, crew scheduling, and operational systems. Pay annual license.  
**OTAs:** Subscribe to real-time fare data and demand forecasts. Pay monthly API fee.  
**GDSes:** Receive unified multi-airline inventory signals. Pay enterprise API fee.  
**TMCs:** Access corporate demand forecasting and route intelligence. Pay monthly fee.
